Joyous Radio

Joyous Radio is a free internet christian radio broadcasting christian devotionals, music and short messages 24/7.
If you wish to contribute your short biblical message or your songs to Joyous Radio. Please contact us at admin@joyousradio.co.za



Presents

Founders of Joyous Radio

Pastor Sipho Mahlangu and his wife, Nthabiseng took Christ literally when He said in Mark 16:15, "Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation". They both are READY to spread the word of God, heal the sick by the Power of the Holy Spirit, help and encourage others.

Their mission is Godbirthed passion to bring people into a saving relationship with Jesus Christ. Pastor Sipho Mahlangu has been evangelizing on the Radio since 2010. His aim in life is to bring glory to his Lord and Savior Jesus Christ by being a faithful witness of him JouousRadio airs Pastor Mahlangu's devotional messages everyday names "Start Your New Day".
